French Fries are thin cut potatos that are deep fried in oil. It is a crunchy side dish that would go well with a hamburger. It is also very simple to make.



Ingredients
  • potatos
  • sea salt
  • oil



    • Directions
    1. Wash the potato
    2. Cut the potato into strips that are consistently sized
    3. Then into the fryer it goes
    4. It takes around 3-4 minutes to fry
    5. After, sprinkle a generous amount of sea salt
    6. Finally, it will be ready to serve
